Frequently Asked Questions About Carpet Cleaning in Brooklyn

How long does professional carpet cleaning take?

Most professional carpet cleaning appointments in Brooklyn take between 1.5 to 3 hours to complete. The exact duration depends on the total square footage and the level of staining present in your home or office.

How often should I have my carpets professionally cleaned?

We recommend scheduling a deep cleaning every 6 to 12 months for Brooklyn residents. Frequent cleaning is essential in New York to remove the heavy urban dust and allergens that settle into fibers from high foot traffic.

Is professional carpet cleaning safe for pets and children?

Yes, our cleaning process is completely safe for both pets and children. Shiny Rhino uses eco-friendly, non-toxic solutions that effectively remove deep-seated dirt without leaving behind any harmful chemical residues or dangerous fumes in your Brooklyn home.

How long until carpets are dry after cleaning?

Carpets typically take 4 to 8 hours to dry completely after a hot water extraction service. Drying times in Brooklyn can vary based on indoor humidity levels and the amount of airflow available throughout the building.

Do you move furniture during cleaning?

Our technicians will carefully move light furniture like chairs, coffee tables, and side tables before we begin cleaning. We ask that you move fragile items and electronics prior to our arrival at your Brooklyn property for safety.

What is the best carpet cleaning method?

Truck-mounted hot water extraction is the gold standard for deep cleaning. This method reaches the base of the carpet pile to extract pollutants, which is particularly effective for the heavy soil loads found in Brooklyn apartments.

How does Brooklyn humidity affect carpet drying?

High humidity in Brooklyn during summer months can slow down the natural evaporation process. We suggest running your air conditioner or using floor fans to circulate air, which helps ensure your carpets dry within the expected timeframe.

Can you remove soot and city grime from carpets?

Yes, we specialize in removing the oily soot and fine particulate matter common in New York urban environments. Our high-temperature steam breaks down these stubborn pollutants, restoring the original color and texture of your carpets effectively.
