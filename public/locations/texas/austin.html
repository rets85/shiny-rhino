Our Cleaning Process

  • Pre-Inspection and Area Assessment: Our IICRC certified technicians evaluate your carpet type and identify specific stains or high-traffic zones that require extra attention before the cleaning begins.
  • Industrial Pre-Vacuuming: We use high-powered vacuums to remove dry soil, pet hair, and loose debris from deep within the fibers. This step prevents mud from forming during the wet extraction phase.
  • Eco-Friendly Pre-Treatment: A specialized, non-toxic cleaning solution is applied to break down oils, grease, and stubborn spots. This solution is safe for families and allows for a deeper clean.
  • Agitation and Soil Suspension: We use professional grooming tools to gently work the pre-treatment into the carpet. This ensures that every fiber is coated and that the dirt is fully suspended for removal.
  • Truck-Mounted Hot Water Extraction: Our powerful truck-mounted equipment injects hot water into the carpet and immediately extracts it. This process removes dirt, bacteria, and allergens while leaving minimal moisture behind.
  • Final Grooming and Rapid Drying: We use specialized carpet rakes to reset the nap and promote faster drying times. Our high-velocity air movers can be used to ensure your floors are ready to walk on quickly.

The Shiny Rhino difference lies in our commitment to using the highest industry standards for every job. By employing IICRC certified professionals, we ensure that your carpets are treated with the correct pH-balanced solutions for their specific material.

Our truck-mounted system is significantly more powerful than portable units used by many competitors. This extra suction power means we remove more water and soil, which prevents the "crunchy" residue and mold growth often associated with DIY cleaning methods.

We prioritize the longevity of your flooring by avoiding harsh chemicals that can strip away factory-applied stain resistors. Our process is designed to leave your Austin home smelling fresh and looking like new without any sticky soap film left behind.

Every step of our process is documented and checked for quality. We take the time to move light furniture and place protective pads underneath to prevent wood stain transfer. This attention to detail is why Austin homeowners trust us with their most valuable rugs.

Our technicians are trained to handle a wide variety of carpet styles, including nylon, polyester, and wool. We adjust our water temperature and pressure settings based on the manufacturer recommendations to protect your carpet warranty and ensure the safest possible clean.

Frequently Asked Questions About Carpet Cleaning in Austin

How long does professional carpet cleaning take?

Most professional carpet cleaning appointments in Austin take between 1.5 to 3 hours to complete. The exact duration depends on the total square footage and the level of soiling present in the fibers. Larger homes in neighborhoods like Westlake or Circle C may require additional time for deep extraction.

How often should I have my carpets professionally cleaned?

We recommend scheduling a professional cleaning every 6 to 12 months to maintain your indoor air quality. In the dusty Austin environment, frequent cleanings help remove allergens and cedar pollen that settle into your flooring. High traffic households with pets or children may benefit from more frequent quarterly visits.

Is professional carpet cleaning safe for pets and children?

Yes, our cleaning processes at Shiny Rhino utilize eco-friendly and non-toxic solutions that are completely safe for your family. We focus on thorough rinsing to ensure no chemical residues are left behind on your Austin home floors. Your children and pets can safely enjoy the carpets immediately after they are dry.

How long until carpets are dry after cleaning?

Carpets typically take 4 to 8 hours to dry completely depending on the humidity levels in Central Texas. Using air conditioning or ceiling fans can significantly speed up the drying process during the humid Austin summer months. We use high powered suction to remove as much moisture as possible during the extraction phase.

Do you move furniture during cleaning?

Our technicians can move light furniture such as chairs, side tables, and coffee tables to ensure a thorough clean. For safety reasons, we do not move heavy items like large entertainment centers, beds, or pianos. We recommend clearing small decorative items and breakables before our team arrives at your Austin residence.

What is the best carpet cleaning method?

Truck-mounted hot water extraction, often called steam cleaning, is the gold standard for deep cleaning carpets. This method is highly effective at removing the fine limestone dust common in the Austin area. It reaches deep into the carpet pile to extract dirt and bacteria that vacuuming cannot reach.

How does the Austin climate affect my carpets?

The high humidity and seasonal pollen counts in Austin can lead to faster soil buildup and potential microbial growth. Allergens like mountain cedar can become trapped in carpet fibers, aggravating respiratory issues for local residents. Regular professional extraction is essential to remove these specific regional environmental contaminants from your home.

Can you remove red dirt stains common in Texas?

Yes, we use specialized spotting agents designed to break down the iron oxide found in Texas red clay soil. While these stains can be stubborn, our hot water extraction process is very effective at lifting red dirt from carpet fibers. Prompt treatment of these stains ensures the best possible restoration results for your flooring.
