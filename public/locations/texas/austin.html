Our Cleaning Process

Our professional cleaning process follows strict industry standards to ensure the best results. We utilize a systematic approach to remove dirt, allergens, and stubborn stains from your flooring.

1. Pre-Inspection: Our technicians walk through your home to identify specific problem areas. We note the carpet type and evaluate any stains that require special attention.

2. Commercial Vacuuming: We use high-powered vacuums to remove dry soil and loose debris. This step is essential for preventing mud from forming during the wet cleaning phase.

3. Pre-Treatment: We apply a specialized cleaning solution to the entire carpet surface. This eco-friendly formula breaks down oils and loosens dirt trapped deep within the fibers.

4. Agitation: Our team uses professional grooming tools to work the solution into the carpet. This ensures that every fiber is thoroughly coated and ready for extraction.

5. Hot Water Extraction: We use truck-mounted equipment to rinse the carpet with hot water. The powerful suction removes the water, cleaning solution, and dissolved dirt simultaneously.

6. Post-Cleaning Grooming: We rake the carpet fibers to help them stand upright and dry faster. This final step leaves your floors looking uniform and professionally finished.

Every technician at Shiny Rhino holds IICRC certification. This means our team is trained in the latest science and techniques for textile and floor care.

We use truck-mounted equipment because it provides superior heat and suction. This professional machinery removes significantly more moisture than portable rental units found at grocery stores.

Higher heat levels allow us to sanitize your carpets without using harsh chemicals. The powerful vacuum system ensures that your floors dry within a few short hours.

Our process is designed to be thorough yet gentle on your home. We take care to protect your walls and furniture while we work in each room.

We use corner guards to prevent our hoses from scuffing your baseboards. Our technicians also wear protective shoe covers to keep your home pristine from start to finish.

The Shiny Rhino difference lies in our attention to detail and commitment to quality. We do not rush through the job because we want your carpets to stay clean longer.

By removing the soapy residue that many DIY machines leave behind, we prevent rapid re-soiling. Your carpets will feel soft and look vibrant for many months to come.

Frequently Asked Questions About Carpet Cleaning in Austin

How long does professional carpet cleaning take?

A standard professional cleaning typically takes about 20 to 30 minutes per room. This timeframe depends on the size of your Austin home and the level of staining present in the fibers.

How often should I have my carpets professionally cleaned?

Most manufacturers recommend professional hot water extraction every 12 to 18 months to maintain your warranty. Austin residents with pets or allergies may benefit from a deeper clean every 6 months.

Is professional carpet cleaning safe for pets and children?

Yes, our cleaning solutions are non-toxic and eco-friendly for your entire family. We use specialized rinsing agents that leave no sticky or harmful residue behind in your carpet fibers.

How long until carpets are dry after cleaning?

Carpets usually take between 4 and 8 hours to dry completely depending on airflow and humidity. In the Austin climate, using your air conditioning or ceiling fans can significantly speed up this drying process.

Do you move furniture during cleaning?

We can move light furniture like chairs, end tables, and coffee tables during our visit. For safety reasons, we do not move heavy items like pianos, large china cabinets, or electronics.

What is the best carpet cleaning method?

Truck-mounted hot water extraction is widely considered the most effective method for deep cleaning. It removes more dirt, bacteria, and allergens than any other residential or commercial cleaning process available today.

How does the Austin humidity affect my carpet cleaning?

High humidity in Central Texas can occasionally extend drying times for deep-cleaned carpets. We use high-powered extraction equipment to remove as much moisture as possible to prevent any musty odors or mold.

Can you remove red dirt and cedar pollen stains?

Our specialized cleaning solutions are designed to tackle common local issues like Texas red dirt and seasonal cedar pollen. We use targeted pre-treatments to break down these stubborn particles before the main extraction begins.
